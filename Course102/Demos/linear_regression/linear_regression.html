一元线性回归

设数据集有 m 个样本 X={x[1],x[2],...,x[m]},Y={y[1],y[2],...,y[m]},x∈R,y∈RX=\{x^{[1]},x^{[2]},...,x^{[m]}\},Y=\{y^{[1]},y^{[2]},...,y^{[m]}\},x\in \mathbb{R},y\in \mathbb{R}X={x[1],x[2],...,x[m]},Y={y[1],y[2],...,y[m]},x∈R,y∈R

模型函数：

y^=a+bx\begin{aligned}\hat{y} = a + bx\end{aligned}y^​=a+bx​

均方差损失函数：

J(a,b)=12mΣi=1m(y^[i]−y[i])2=12mΣi=1m(a+bx[i]−y[i])2\begin{aligned} J(a,b)&=\frac{1}{2m}\Sigma_{i=1} ^{m}(\hat{y}^{[i]}-y^{[i]})^2 \\ &=\frac{1}{2m}\Sigma_{i=1}^{m}(a+bx^{[i]}-y^{[i]})^2 \end{aligned}J(a,b)​=2m1​Σi=1m​(y^​[i]−y[i])2=2m1​Σi=1m​(a+bx[i]−y[i])2​

损失函数在a，b方向上的偏导数：

a:dJ(a,b)da=1mΣi=1m(a+bx[i]−y[i])b:dJ(a,b)db=1mΣi=1m(a+bx[i]−y[i])∗x[i]\begin{aligned} a: \frac{dJ(a,b)}{da}=&\frac{1}{m}\Sigma_{i=1}^m(a+bx^{[i]}-y^{[i]}) \\ b: \frac{dJ(a,b)}{db}=&\frac{1}{m}\Sigma_{i=1}^m(a+bx^{[i]}-y^{[i]}) *x^{[i]} \end{aligned}a:dadJ(a,b)​=b:dbdJ(a,b)​=​m1​Σi=1m​(a+bx[i]−y[i])m1​Σi=1m​(a+bx[i]−y[i])∗x[i]​

最小二乘法求解：

J(a,b) 是一个凸函数，当 J(a,b) 的偏导数为0时达到极值

{dJ(a,b)da=0dJ(a,b)db=0得出：{a=1mΣi=1my[i]−bmΣi=1mx[i]b=Σi=1mx[i]y[i]−1mΣi=1mx[i]Σi=1my[i]Σi=1mx[i]2−1mΣi=1mx[i]Σi=1mx[i]\begin{aligned}& \left\{\begin{aligned} \frac{dJ(a,b)}{da}&=0 \\ \frac{dJ(a,b)}{db}&=0 \end{aligned}\right. \\ 得出：\\ &\left\{\begin{aligned} a &= \frac{1}{m}\Sigma_{i=1}^my^{[i]}-\frac{b}{m}\Sigma_{i=1}^mx^{[i]} \\ b &= \frac{\Sigma_{i=1}^mx^{[i]}y^{[i]}-\frac{1}{m}\Sigma_{i=1}^mx^{[i]}\Sigma_{i=1}^my^{[i]}}{\Sigma_{i=1}^m{x^{[i]}}^2-\frac{1}{m}\Sigma_{i=1}^mx^{[i]}\Sigma_{i=1}^mx^{[i]}} \end{aligned}\right. \end{aligned}得出：​⎩⎨⎧​dadJ(a,b)​dbdJ(a,b)​​=0=0​⎩⎨⎧​ab​=m1​Σi=1m​y[i]−mb​Σi=1m​x[i]=Σi=1m​x[i]2−m1​Σi=1m​x[i]Σi=1m​x[i]Σi=1m​x[i]y[i]−m1​Σi=1m​x[i]Σi=1m​y[i]​​​

梯度下降法求解：

参数延着逆梯度方向下降，逐步逼近损失函数的极小值

设学习率为 η\etaη：

a=a−η∗dJ(a,b)dab=b−η∗dJ(a,b)db\begin{aligned} a &= a - \eta * \frac{dJ(a,b)}{da} \\ b &= b - \eta * \frac{dJ(a,b)}{db} \end{aligned}ab​=a−η∗dadJ(a,b)​=b−η∗dbdJ(a,b)​​

多元线性回归

设数据集有 m 个样本 X={X[1],X[2],...,X[m]},Y={y[1],y[2],...,y[m]},X[i]∈Rn,y[i]∈RX=\{X^{[1]},X^{[2]},...,X^{[m]}\},Y=\{y^{[1]},y^{[2]},...,y^{[m]}\},X^{[i]}\in \mathbb{R^n},y^{[i]}\in \mathbb{R}X={X[1],X[2],...,X[m]},Y={y[1],y[2],...,y[m]},X[i]∈Rn,y[i]∈R

模型函数:

y^[i]=θ0x0[i]+θ1x1[i]+θ2x2[i]+...+θnxn[i]=ΘTX[i]\begin{aligned} \hat{y}^{[i]} &= \theta_0x_0^{[i]} + \theta_1x_1^{[i]}+\theta_2x_2^{[i]}+...+\theta_n x_n^{[i]} \\ &=\Theta^TX^{[i]} \end{aligned}y^​[i]​=θ0​x0[i]​+θ1​x1[i]​+θ2​x2[i]​+...+θn​xn[i]​=ΘTX[i]​

均方差损失函数:
